Who is Tomorrow by Together?

Txt also known as Tomorrow by Togetjer is a 5-member kpop boygroup that was formed under BIGIT MUSIC which is a part of HYBE labels. The groups' five members are Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Hueningkai! The group made their debut on March 4th, 2019. With their debut they released their first mini album called "The Dream Chapter: STAR." In the group there are not any set positions of the members, instead it often changed with each comeback, release and song.

The groups' name means 'Different people coming together under one dream in hopes of buidling a better tomorrow'. When the group greets themselves they always say "One! Dream! Hello we are TXT!" Since their debut in 2019 some of the members of branched off to work on their solo music as well as still participating in group activities. This included Yeonjun's first single "Ggum" and Beomgyu following with his first solo single "Panic"!
